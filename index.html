About us

Established in 2001, Haircombe has been a family-business from the start. We have built a reputation of giving the best quality service along with a warm welcome and a feel at home atmoshpere. Our team of experienced barbers will offer you the best service you can get. Besides a perfect haircut, clients can also have a luxury straight razor wet shave or a classic beard and mustache treatment.
